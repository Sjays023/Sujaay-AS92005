Greenhouse gases are gases in the air which trap heat radiated from the sun, this keeps the Earth warm. There are many types of greenhouse gases which vary from carbon dioxide (CO2), methane (CH4), nitrous oxide (N2O), and fluorinated gases (F-gases). These gases trap heat in the atmosphere leading to a warmer planet
